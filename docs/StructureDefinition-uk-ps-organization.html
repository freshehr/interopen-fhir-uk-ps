Resource Profile: UK PS Organization

Official URL: http://hl7.org.uk/fhir/ps/StructureDefinition/uk-ps-organization Version: 0.1.0-cibuild
Standards status: Draft Maturity Level: 1 Computable Name: UKPSOrganization

Copyright/Legal: Used by permission of HL7 International, all rights reserved Creative Commons License. HL7 UK© 2025+; Licensed Under Creative Commons No Rights Reserved.

​This profile sets minimum expectations for an Organization resource in the context of a patient summary in a UK context. It is based on the UKCore Organization profile, and applies the additional requirements from Organization (IPS).

Usages:

  • Use this Profile: UK PS Bundle
  • Refer to this Profile: UK PS Composition, UK PS Encounter, UK PS MedicationRequest, UK PS Patient and UK PS PractitionerRole

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics
