Other Profiles Used

In addition to the profiles defined in this implementation guide, the following profiles defined elsewhere are used by AU Patient Summary. These profiles are a data type profile for a Must Support element in an AU PS profile or are the target of a Must Support reference element in an AU PS profile through definition or by impose:

  • AU Australian Business Number
  • AU Base Dosage
  • AU Core Location
  • AU DVA Number
  • AU HPI-I
  • AU HPI-O
  • AU IHI
  • AU Medicare Card Number
  • AU Medicare Provider Number
  • Codeable Concept (IPS)
  • Device (IPS)
  • DeviceUseStatement (IPS)
  • DiagnosticReport (IPS)
  • Observation Results - Radiology (IPS)

​Additional IPS profiles are referenced in AU PS Bundle and AU PS Composition, but are not labelled Must Support in AU PS (or IPS).

Extensions

All extensions used in this guide are defined in the FHIR Extensions Pack or AU Base.

The following extensions are marked with Must Support in this implementation guide:

  • ​Australian Indigenous Status in AU PS Patient
  • ​Individual Pronouns in AU PS Patient
  • ​Individual Gender Identity in AU PS Patient
